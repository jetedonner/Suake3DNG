Suake3D Help

Introduction

Suake3D is a clone of the classic 2D game Snake known from the old Nokia cell phones made up in 3D. It implements the theme and features of the 3D ego shooter Quake and allows you to fight your enemies in a 3D world.

Gameplay and goal

The main goal of the game is to catch as many goodies as you can to score points and surpass your opponent suakes. You also have to avoid to get shot by your enemies namely your opponents and the droids that are also running around in the game area and are trying to get you. If you drain health by hits from your enemies you can regenerate your health by collecting medkits which are spread around the level but be fast your enemies don't sleep.
